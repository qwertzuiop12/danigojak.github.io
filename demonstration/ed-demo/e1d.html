About E&D International

E&D International Consulting & Trading Inc. is a trusted immigration consulting firm in Hamilton, Ontario. Founded and led by Ernad Causevic, the firm is known in the community for professional guidance, reliable service, and a personal approach to immigration matters.

The firm has built its reputation by helping people understand their options clearly, organize their applications properly, and move through the immigration process with more confidence. Clients come to E&D for both experience and honest communication.
