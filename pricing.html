Terms of Service

Service Description

Our web services include custom design, development, hosting, maintenance, and support as outlined in your selected plan. The one-time design fee covers all initial creative work, while the recurring subscription covers ongoing operational costs.

Payment Terms

The one-time design fee is due before work begins. Recurring subscriptions are billed according to your selected cycle (monthly or yearly). Monthly plans auto-bill each month until cancelled. Yearly plans auto-renew at the discounted rate unless cancelled with 30 days notice.

Automatic Renewal

All subscriptions automatically renew at the end of each billing cycle. You will be notified by email before each renewal. To cancel, please provide written notice at least 30 days before your renewal date.

Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your subscription at any time, but no refunds are provided for partial billing periods. Upon cancellation, your website will remain active until the end of your current billing period.

Refund Policy

The one-time design fee is non-refundable once work has begun. Subscription fees are non-refundable except as required by law. We offer a 14-day satisfaction guarantee for new clients.

Service Level Agreement

We guarantee 99.9% uptime for hosting services. Support response times vary by plan: Starter (48 business hours), Growth (24 business hours), Enterprise (12 business hours).

Data & Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including SSL encryption, regular backups, and software updates. Client data is never shared with third parties except as required to provide services.

Liability

Our liability is limited to the fees paid for services. We are not liable for indirect, incidental, or consequential damages. Clients are responsible for maintaining their own content backups.

Why Our Pricing Model Works

Transparent, fair, and designed for long-term success.

Fair Pricing

You pay once for the creative work (design/development) and a separate ongoing fee for the actual costs of keeping your site running securely.

No Surprises

The monthly fee covers everything needed to keep your site live and secure—no hidden costs for domains, hosting, or SSL certificates.

Sustainable Service

Recurring revenue allows us to provide continuous updates, security patches, and support—not just build and abandon.

Price Lock Guarantee

Your monthly rate won't increase for at least 3 years. Yearly subscribers are guaranteed their rate for the duration of their subscription.
