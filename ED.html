About E&D International

E & D International Consulting & Trading Inc. is a trusted immigration consulting firm based in Hamilton, Ontario, with over 25 years of experience helping people navigate the Canadian immigration process. Founded and led by Ernad Causevic, the company is known locally as "ED Immigration" and operates from offices on 87 Parker Ave, Ancaster, ON L9G 1A9, Canada.

Fully regulated by the College of Immigration and Citizenship Consultants (CICC), E & D has built a strong reputation for personalized, reliable guidance. The team includes trained professionals and interns like Savanni Semple, who assist with legal and documentation work. With friendly service, real community roots, and a straightforward approach, E & D International is a go-to for individuals and families looking to make Canada their home.
